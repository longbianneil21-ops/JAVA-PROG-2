Step-by-Step Guide for QCUCAT Application


The Quezon City University (QCU) Admissions Office announces the guidelines and requirements for the QCU College Admission Test (QCUCAT) for Academic Year 2026–2027
All applicants are advised to carefully read the instructions and prepare the necessary scanned documents before registration.


Prepare Scanned Documents Before Registration
All applicants are advised to prepare clear and readable scanned copies of the following credentials in PDF format before proceeding with registration. 


Fill out the QCU Student Application form:

2. Submit Valid School ID and 3 Specimen Signatures Scan both sides (front and back) of your recent school ID. 
Attach three (3) specimen signatures. If not currently enrolled, attach a 2x2 colored photo (white background with name tag and signature). 


Use this file name format:
 lastname.firstname.middlename


 Example: DelaCruz.Juan.Filipinas




Photo Requirements for QCUCAT Application.
 
recent 2x2 colored photo (white background, printed on good-quality photo paper) taken within the last month.


With name tag and signature over printed full name (first name, middle initial, last name)
 e.g. JUANA A. DELA CRUZ. 


It must be a close-up, full-face shot, in formal attire, with no eyeglasses, filters, or accessories covering the face or ears.






Create a Gmail Account


Applicants must create a Gmail account using either of the following formats:


lastname.firstname.middlename@gmail.com
lastname.firstname.middlename1@gmail.com




Submit the following requirements to the Admissions Office (San Bartolome Campus) to get an Examination Permit:


Two (2) sets printout of uploaded and fully accomplished Application Form and Data Consent Form (3 pages, in long bond paper) with 2x2 colored picture (white background with name tag and signature).


Forms may be downloaded (Please refer to Step No. 1) from www.qcu.edu.ph or the QCU Registrar and Admission Division Facebook page.


Check your email for confirmation of successful registration.
On the Examination day, bring your Admission Test Slip, current school ID, and an additional valid ID for emergency purposes.
